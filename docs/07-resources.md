## Resource Types

### Water

| Source | Amount | Crossing | Notes |
|--------|--------|----------|-------|
| Water well (building) | Unlimited | No | Useful in deserts. Size: 1x1x1 |
| Fresh water (river, lake) | Unlimited | Shallow river: Yes, Deep river: No, Lake: No, Frozen lake: Yes | -- |
| Water hole | 50 water each | Yes | Appears randomly after each rainfall on empty land |

### Wood

| Source | Crossing | Notes |
|--------|----------|-------|
| Fallen trees | No | Must cut standing trees first |
| Destroyed buildings | No | Yields wood from structure |

### Stone

| Source | Amount | Crossing | Notes |
|--------|--------|----------|-------|
| Small stone (on land) | Direct gathering | Yes | Workers can gather immediately |
| Small stone (from destroyed buildings) | Equal to building's stone cost | Yes | Direct gathering |
| Big stone | Requires cut stone action | No | Cannot gather without cutting first |

### Clay

- Source: Land deposits
- Amount: **300 clay** per section
- Crossing: **Yes**

### Dead Animals (Meat)

- Both male and female workers can hunt wild animals
- Each animal yields a specific amount of food
- Crossing: **No**

### Fish

- Both male and female workers can fish
- Each fish: **5 food**
- Crossing: **Yes**

### Farm (Food)

- Crossing: **Yes**
- See [Buildings - Farm](./09-buildings.md#farm) for details

### Strawberry Bushes

- Crossing: **No**
- See [GAIA - Fruit Bushes](./06-gaia.md#fruit-bushes-strawberry)

### Animal Skin

- Obtained from hunted animals
- **Requires Skinning upgrade** to gather
- Gathered automatically along with food if upgrade is active

### Long Grass

- Purpose: creating **rope** (sole use)
- 5 units of long grass automatically convert to 1 rope (if Rope upgrade is researched)
- Units can walk through (Crossing: **Yes**)
- Spawns automatically and randomly on any empty land
- Each long grass: **10 grass** resource

---

## Derived Resources

### Supply

- **1 food + 1 water = 1 supply** (automatic conversion)
- Each alive unit and active building consumes a specific amount of supply per time period (e.g., 1 per 10s)
- Total supply and resources are **shared between all supply depots**

### Rope

- **5 long grass = 1 rope** (automatic conversion at home base, requires Rope upgrade)
- Used for: sling upgrade, destroying wooden walls/buildings, boat construction, tree cutting, etc.
